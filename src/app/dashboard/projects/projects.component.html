Our Projects and Works

IMAM MEHDI HOPE EDUCATIONAL AND WELFARE SOCIETY is dedicated to providing assistance to poor children who face various challenges in their lives. Recognizing that poverty can severely impact a child's well-being and future prospects, the NGO aims to address their needs and create opportunities for a brighter future

The assistance provided by the NGO to poor children encompasses several areas, including:

Basic Needs: The NGO ensures that poor and orphan children and old age people have access to their basic needs such as food, clothing, and shelter. They work diligently to provide nutritious meals, suitable clothing, and safe living conditions, creating a nurturing environment for the children to grow and thrive.

Education: Recognizing the transformative power of education, the NGO focuses on providing educational opportunities to poor and orphan children. They establish schools, learning centers, and educational programs specifically designed to meet the needs of these children. By offering quality education, the NGO aims to break the cycle of poverty and empower them to create a better future for themselves.

Free Medical Camp: Our program for free medical camp provides free medical check-ups and medical advice to underprivileged people. We provide basic medical facilities such as diagnostics, treatments and medicines free of cost. We also organize awareness programs to educate people about the importance of healthcare.

The camp is conducted in collaboration with local healthcare professionals and volunteers who are committed to providing high quality medical care to people who cannot afford it. Our program is aimed at reducing the financial burden of the people and improving access to quality healthcare.

Free Ration DistributionIMAM MEHDI HOPE EDUCATIONAL AND WELFARE SOCIETY launched a Ration Distribution Program to provide food and other essential items to those in need. The program seeks to provide basic items such as cereals, pulses, oil, salt, sugar, lentils, tea, spices, and other food items to people in need. The program also provides other essential items such as utensils, blankets, and clothing to those who are unable to purchase them.

The program is aimed at providing food and other essential items to those most in need. It is also intended to provide relief to the most vulnerable sections of the society who are unable to purchase food items due to their financial constraints. The program is funded by the government and other private donors.

The program is implemented through a network of volunteers and organizations working in various parts of the country. The volunteers and organizations are responsible for the distribution of the food items and other essential items to the needy people. The volunteers are responsible for the collection of the food items from the donors and then distribution of the same to the needy people.

Old Age People Welfare ActivityIMAM MEHDI HOPE EDUCATIONAL AND WELFARE SOCIETY has recently launched a special program to provide welfare services to elderly people in the city. The program is aimed at providing health, nutrition, shelter, and other essential services to elderly people in need. The program also provides financial assistance to those who are unable to meet their daily needs. The program also includes workshops and seminars to create awareness about the importance of caring for elderly people. The Society also organizes various events and activities to engage elderly people in the community and ensure their well-being, the Society also provides counseling services to elderly people to help them cope with the various challenges of aging.

Annual Function & Celebration: IMAM MEHDI HOPE EDUCATIONAL AND WELFARE SOCIETY (IMHEWS) is a non-governmental organization (NGO) based in Ambedkar Nagar, India. It is dedicated to providing social and educational services to the underprivileged sections of the society

The organization has launched a number of programs to celebrate the important national days such as Republic Day and Independence Day. The primary objective of IMHEWS is to provide education and create awareness among the citizens, especially those living in the rural and urban slums. The organization runs various activities such as health camps, vocational training, computer classes, and awareness programs.

The Independence Day celebration program includes cultural activities, patriotic songs, and folk dance performances. IMHEWS also provides guidance to the children and their families on various topics related to the nation's history and culture. IMHEWS also organizes an annual function every year in which it felicitates the achievers from the community. It also honors the volunteers who have contributed to the success of the organization.
